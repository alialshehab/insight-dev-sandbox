Simulated real-time geolocation data from users in NYC

The above map displays a simulated real time stream of geolocation data from users walking around NYC. A stream processing framework (Apache Storm) is used for transforming streaming data such that you can see all users or just free users. The goal of this app is to help people hangout by providing a framework that enables users to view real time location and availability status of thier friends
